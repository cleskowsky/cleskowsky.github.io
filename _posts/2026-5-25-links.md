* [The VibeSec Reckoning](https://martinfowler.com/articles/vibesec-reckoning.html): Consider including security rules
  just after the system prompt in a harness. (Read @secure_coding_guidelines.md in claude.md for e.g.) Prompting isn't
  enough, though. Feedback and signals from other tools should be added to a conversation, and objective quality gates
  used.
* [Liz Fong-Jones - AI is like Chocolate (and Observability is like Strawberries)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zGr8mAar_qw&list=WL&index=45):
  Putting ai into everything is like trying to put chocolate into everything. It doesn't work. Skills don't go away -
  people still need to know how things work because we have to support and maintain the things we build.
